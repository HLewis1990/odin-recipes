Mario Burger



The Mario burger is an all-beef burger with bacon, mushrooms, lettuce, tomato, American cheese, and a side of truffle fries. Visually, the burger is one of the quirkiest menu items you can order. The burger tasted pretty average to us, though. The brioche bun was a bit too big for the patty and the beef was on the dry side.


It's a real Mama Mia Mania favorite
